39 lesser extent, (e) the linguistic content of the message is not

41 recognition techniques, whether human or automatic, to account for these
42 uncontrolled factors. Under more constrained or calibrated situations,
43 or as an aid for investigative purposes, judicious application of these
44 techniques may be suitable, provided they are not considered as infallible.
